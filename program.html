Our Programmes

Strengthening Community Structures for Resilience Community Managed Sustainable Livelihoods Community Based Care for Children with Disabilities Education Sponsorship and Advocacy Gender and Environment

Strengthening Community Structures for Resilience

This program focuses on building the capacity of community structures to enhance resilience. We work with local leaders and volunteers to develop sustainable solutions that address social, economic, and environmental challenges.

Community Managed Sustainable Livelihoods

Through this program, we empower communities to manage their livelihoods sustainably. We provide training, resources, and support to help them diversify income streams and improve food security.

Community Based Care for Children with Disabilities

This program aims to improve the quality of life for children with disabilities by providing community-based care and support. We work with families and local organizations to ensure inclusive and accessible services.

Education Sponsorship and Advocacy

We sponsor education for vulnerable children and advocate for policies that promote access to quality education. Our goal is to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn and thrive.

Gender and Environment

This program promotes gender equality and environmental sustainability. We empower women and girls to take leadership roles in environmental conservation and climate change adaptation.
